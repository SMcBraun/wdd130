Temple History

The Laie Hawaii Temple holds a unique place in Church history as the first temple built outside the continental United States. Dedicated on November 27, 1919 by Heber J Grant, it's the oldest operating temple outside of Utah and the fifth oldest temple still in operation worldwide. This sacred edifice brought the blessings of temple ordinances closer to the members of the Pacific, serving as a beacon of faith and devotion for generations.
